Mission

Purpose: This project will be a professional website portfolio with an emphasis on web development tools gained and honed during this bootcamp as well as prior leadership and technical experience.

Audience: This site is designed with a professional audience in mind, to include professional peers, other developers, potential employers, customers, and clients.

Goal: The primary goal of this site is to acquire full-time employment and/or attract freelance clients, including a user-friendly contact form as well as links to social media profiles. The secondary goal is to serve as a gallery to showcase and promote my work.

Persona

Name: Jacob Davidson

Age: 34

Profession: He is a junior partner of a small law firm. Recently his mentor, the owner of the firm, passed away, leaving an estate to fight over, a business to run, and decades worth of clients to reconcile with. Currently, his main method of attracting new clientele is using social media and word-of-mouth. However, he would like a more centralized site that accurately represents his work, client testimonials, and of course supplies a user-friendly contact form, as well as a way for potential partners and employees to reach out.

Technology: He is an average user with outdated PCs and a laptop running Windows 10 and an Android Smart phone. He uses technology to stay connected with news and social media. He is especially active on Facebook and LinkedIn.

Attitudes and Behaviors: Short on time, overworked, and also has personal drama to contend with. He often misses phone appointments and social events due to being run down and overwhelmed.

Frustrations and Needs: He is frustrated by how jam-packed his schedule is and feels like he has to do everything himself for it to be done right. He needs to automate some of the processes that he generally performs manually.

Goals: Jacob envisions that a business website will provide additional tools to streamline his business workflow. He has identified that he spends a majority of his time communicating, and is hoping there is a way to get some of that time back in the realm of technology. Additionally, he is hoping to vet potential partners to help balance his workload and perhaps expand his business capabilities, so some type of job placement forms on his site would be essential.

User Story: Jacob is an excellent lawyer, and generally an intelligent person, but he has always depended on his own talent and hard work to perform the functions that make him successful. What he is looking for is someone he trusts that is tech savvy to analyze his workflow and find analogs that a computer could perform.
