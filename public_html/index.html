A little bit about me

I am proud to have served over 10 years in the United States Navy, where I acquired invaluable experience and wore many hats: leader, manager, mentor, instructor, inspector, problem-solver, liaison, technician, scholar, voyager, and friend... to name just a few. But I can't describe how excited I am to take the next step in this journey, to pursue a love that predates my career of launching jets from the decks of aircraft carriers. Coding has always felt a bit like magic to me, a way to radically expand a human mind's ability to solve complex problems. Share this adventure with me as we team up to do great things.
